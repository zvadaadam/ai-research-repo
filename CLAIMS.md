## Claim Rules

Use `supported`, `weakened`, `mixed`, `superseded`, or `unknown`.

Every claim should link to concrete evidence:

```text
E### experiment
R### run
P### paper
D### decision
```

If a claim has no evidence links, it is a hypothesis and belongs in an
experiment card or `OPEN_QUESTIONS.md`, not here.
